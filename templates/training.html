Training

Use the hint model, not random luck.

Opening move

Use a balanced unique-digit opener like 428 or 571. This reveals whether you should keep digits, move them, or clear them entirely.

Mid-game discipline

When you get one exact match, freeze that position and rotate the other digits. When you get only misplaced digits, permute positions before introducing new numbers.

Timer runs

In timer mode, stop brute-forcing. Make one structural change per guess so each hint removes uncertainty instead of adding it.

Daily challenge

The daily secret is deterministic per date, so every player gets the same puzzle. Your preview hint should shape the first guess.
