How to Play

Learn the strategy and beat the scoreboard.

Step-by-Step Rules

1 The game picks a secret number between 000 and 999.
2 You have 10 attempts to guess the number.
3 Each guess returns feedback: Too High, Too Low, or Correct.
4 Use logic to narrow the range and win in fewer attempts.

Pro Tips

1 Start around 500 to split the range in half.
2 After each hint, guess the midpoint of the remaining range.
3 Track your guesses so you never repeat a number.
